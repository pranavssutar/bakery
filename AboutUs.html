Crumbs and Flakess

In the world of baking, the magic always starts with one crumb and one flake. The combination of carefully crafted, infinite number of crumbs and flakes, always results in "melt-in-the-mouth delicacies" Crumbs and Flakess, is an effort combined with love and passion to bring on the table some of the most delectable baked delicacies. It is founded by Usri Sen, who has always been a marketing professional but interestingly always found herself gravitating towards the magical World of Flour, Butter and Sugar. Our bakery is beautiful to behold, and is always bursting with flavors and textures. Everything is made from scratch only post an order is placed so as to deliver the freshest product possible. The menu is carefully crafted putting together some of the World delicacies.
